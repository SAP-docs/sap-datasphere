# Integrating Data from SAP BW and SAP BW/4HANA with the Data Product Generator for SAP Business Data Cloud

In the SAP Datasphere component of SAP Business Data Cloud, you can work with data and objects received from SAP BW and SAP BW/4HANA systems via the Data Product Generator for SAP Business Data Cloud.

For more information on the Data Product Generator, see [Integrating Data from the Data Product Generator for SAP Business Data Cloud](https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_BW4HANA/107a6e8a38b74ede94c833ca3b7b6f51/ec54590916674554b61efa61a5bb4ba0.html) \(SAP BW/4HANA documentation\).
